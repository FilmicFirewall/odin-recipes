Pasta

Description

Pasta comes in different forms and my favorite pasta recipe is to make an Alfredo Pasta.

It is both yum and creamy. Tastes like heaven and you won't regret making it for yourself and your family.

Ingredients

  • Macroni
  • Capsicum
  • Chicken
  • Soya Sauce
  • Chilli Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni
  2. Add capsicum
  3. Then add chicken to it
  4. Add sauces such as chilli and soya sauce
  5. For seasoning, add black pepper and chilli flakes
  6. Pasta is ready to be served
